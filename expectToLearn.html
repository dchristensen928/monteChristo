Skills You'll Learn

Living on a working ranch and homestead for a year will teach you many invaluable skills that can only be learned through practice. You will have ample opportunity to learn skills related to the production of home-grown food, animal husbandry, traditional trades, and more.

Ranch Skills

some of the ranch skils you'll learn include:

  • fence construction
  • irrigation and hay-making
  • milking and dairy production
  • butchering of whole beef, sheep, pork, and wild game
  • Western horsemanship
  • livestock nutrition and husbandry

Outdoorsmanship

In addition to learning essential ranch and homestead skills, the intern can also expect to learn and practice the following outdoors sports:

  • fly fishing and fly tying
  • traditional archery
  • rifle safety and marksmanship
  • hiking and land navigation

Tannery

You will also find yourself helping at the small natural tannery located on the ranch. Interns will get to tan and take home their own

  • Sheep Pelt
  • Sheep Leather
  • Traditional Buckskin
