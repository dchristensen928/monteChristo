Skills You'll Learn

Living on a working ranch and homestead for a year will teach you many invaluable skills that can only be learned through practice. You will have ample opportunity to...

Ranch Skills

Some of the ranch skills you'll learn include:

  • Fence construction
  • Irrigation and hay-making
  • Milking and dairy production
  • Butchering of whole beef, sheep, pork, and wild game
  • Western horsemanship
  • Livestock nutrition and husbandry

Outdoorsmanship

In addition to learning essential ranch and homestead skills, the intern can also expect to learn and practice the following outdoor sports:

  • Fly fishing and fly tying
  • Traditional archery
  • Rifle safety and marksmanship
  • Hiking and land navigation

Tannery

You will also find yourself helping at the small natural tannery located on the ranch. Interns will get to tan and take home their own:

  • Sheep Pelt
  • Sheep Leather
  • Traditional Buckskin
