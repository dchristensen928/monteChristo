We live "in sync" with the natural world around us. That means that we are not in a hurry to accomplish tasks. When we do the right activities at the right time, stress is reduced, and quality of life improves. "There is a time for everything under the sun" (Eccl 3:1). Our job is to make our daily activities correspond with the natual and liturgical environment we are immersed in.
